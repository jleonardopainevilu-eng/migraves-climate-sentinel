## Positioning statement

MigrAves does not claim to invent edge AI, acoustic bird recognition, animal telemetry, ecological digital twins, or climate bioindicators. Its proposed differentiation is an operational and educational data pipeline that defines local ecological areas, combines biological and environmental evidence, generates a standardized climate-response record, exposes provenance and uncertainty, requires human validation, and exports interoperable research formats.
